DIRECTIVE — FOREX BASELINE TREND REVERSAL (GBPUSD)

Family: Forex
Strategy Name: Baseline_Trend_Reversal_4C
Symbol: EURUSD
Broker: OctaFX
Timeframe: 1D (Daily)

Test Window

Start Date: 2020-01-01

End Date: 2025-12-31

Candle Definitions

GREEN candle: Close > Open

RED candle: Close < Open

Doji (Close == Open):

Breaks consecutive count

Invalidates entry/exit sequence

Entry Logic (Executed at SIGNAL CANDLE CLOSE)
Long Entry

Enter LONG when all conditions are met:

Four (4) consecutive RED daily candles

Execution:

Enter LONG at the CLOSE of the 4th RED candle

Short Entry

Enter SHORT when all conditions are met:

Four (4) consecutive GREEN daily candles

Execution:

Enter SHORT at the CLOSE of the 4th GREEN candle

Exit Logic
Primary Exit — Stop & Reverse (Executed at SIGNAL CANDLE CLOSE)

The strategy maintains at most one open position.

If LONG:

On completion of four (4) consecutive GREEN candles:

Close LONG

Open SHORT at the CLOSE of the 4th GREEN candle

If SHORT:

On completion of four (4) consecutive RED candles:

Close SHORT

Open LONG at the CLOSE of the 4th RED candle

Fail-Safe Risk Control

Hard Stop:

500 pips adverse movement from entry price

Stop is intraday-active

On stop hit:

Position is closed immediately

No stop-and-reverse

System waits for the next valid entry sequence

Positioning Rules

Single position only (no pyramiding)

No partial exits

Signals evaluated only on fully closed daily candles

Stop & Reverse has priority over fresh entries

Execution Compatibility Declaration

Execution timing is CLOSE-OF-BAR

Directive is fully compatible with
Universal_Research_Engine v1.2.0

No next-bar or OPEN execution is required

Governance Notes

No indicators beyond OHLC candles

All metrics computed in Stage-1 only

No parameter optimization permitted

End of Directive
