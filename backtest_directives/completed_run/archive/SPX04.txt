ENTRY LOGIC
Direction: Long only
Condition:
Enter long when Close < (Highest High of the previous 5 completed bars − ATR(10))

EXIT LOGIC (OR precedence — whichever occurs first)

1. Price Exit (Dynamic):
   On each bar close, recompute:
   HH_prev = Highest High of the previous 5 completed bars
   Exit when Close > HH_prev

2. Time Exit:
   Exit when Bars Held ≥ 4

Single position only (no pyramiding)
No short trades
Entry only when flat
Exit resets position to flat
Bar held must be tracked internally
