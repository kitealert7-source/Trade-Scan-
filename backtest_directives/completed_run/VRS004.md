## Regime Detection

Price above EMA of 200 period
EMA slope > 0 over last 20 bars

Uptrend permission = ( price > EMA 200 and EMA slope > 0 over last 20 bars )

Downtrend permission = ( price < EMA 200 and EMA slope < 0 over last 20 bars)

## Entry

RSI Based
Long: RSI(2) average of (T-1, T-2) ≤ 25 AND Uptrend permission
Short: RSI(2) average of (T-1, T-2) ≥ 75 AND Downtrend permission



## Exit

**Exit Logic (Precedence):**

* **PRIMARY:** RSI(2) > 75 or RSI(2) < 25 (exhaustion exit)
* **FALLBACK:** Bars held >= 15 (timeout)
* **STOP:** 1.5 % of entry price
* Take Profit: 2.0 R fixed risk-reward ratio



## Risk Management

**HARD STOP:** 2% of entry price (fail-safe) OVER RIDES STOP IN CASE OF GAP DOWNS
