Core Logic: Opening Range Breakout

Entry Rules:

  Use M5 data to compute:

• Order Placement (at 06:00 UTC):
  Place:
      - Buy Stop at Session High
      - Sell Stop at Session Low

  - Maximum two trades per day.
  - No re-entry after second trade.

• Time-Based Exit:
  Close all open positions at 18:00 UTC.

• Stop Loss:
