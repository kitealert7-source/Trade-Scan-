# /contemplate

Assume the design has already survived normal deliberation.

Pause and ask:

- Are we solving the right problem?
- Have we mistaken sufficiency for completeness?
- What important assumption or perspective might still be missing?
- Is there any reasoned concern that genuinely deserves attention?

Report only what emerges from reflection. Describe the consideration and why it merits attention. Change nothing.
