## Methodology notes

- Triggers come from `cointegration_triggers` populated daily by the screener.
- Forward walk: rows in `cointegration_daily` with `as_of > trigger.as_of`,
  same (pair_a, pair_b, lookback_days), up to 60 bars.
- Reversion: first bar where |current_zscore| ≤ 1.0.
- Direction inference: LONG_SPREAD when z<0, SHORT_SPREAD when z>0.
- Asset-class membership uses the same FX/IDX/CC sets as the screener's `classify_pair`.
